Privacy Policy


The Tyler Simmons Team at Coldwell Banker Realty takes privacy seriously and is committed to protecting the personal information of its website users. This privacy statement applies to the information collected through our website and outlines our data collection, use, and protection practices.

1. Collection of Information

We collect personal information from our users, including but not limited to, name, email address, and phone number, in order to provide better service and communication. This information is voluntarily provided by users through forms or online interactions.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used to provide you with the services you request and to keep you informed of our services, real estate market updates, and upcoming events. We may also use your phone number to send text messages related to our services and real estate market updates. By calling or texting this number (803) 999-7522, customers agree to receive text messages. If you no longer wish to receive text messages, you may opt out at any time by replying "STOP".

3. Protection of Information

The Tyler Simmons Team at Coldwell Banker Realty takes appropriate measures to secure and protect the information we collect. Access to personal information is limited to authorized personnel who need it to perform their job responsibilities.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share personal information to third parties. Information may be shared with third parties only if required by law or necessary to provide you with the services you request.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as it is necessary to provide you with the services you request or as required by law.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

The Tyler Simmons Team at Coldwell Banker Realty reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated privacy policy on our website.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at admin@isllscre.com or by phone at (803) 999-7522.
